About Me...

I'm Santiago Feas, a 23 year old dude from the city of Caseros, Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I've always been passionate about tech and all it's ramifications, everything from music and video players and recorders, gaming consoles, pc's, cars, etc. What interested me the most was the interfaces and machines design, I wanted to know what it took for these things to be brought to life.

My first approach on this subject was to study Image and Sound Design, where I got to learn about movie producing, video editing and audio mixing and master. Throughout the third year of college, I had the opportunity to attend to a javascrypt lesson, and I inmediately fell in love with it, I wanted to learn more about it, about the idea of being able to create a thing from scratch that can look and behave the way I tell it to.

Since then, I started to look through a bunch of online courses and ended up joining a coding academy, hoping to keep collecting knowledge and become a full stack developer to start creating my own things.
